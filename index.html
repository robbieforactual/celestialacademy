Who We Are

Celestial Jiu-Jitsu Academy opened its doors in early 2020 in Portobello and has since relocated to new premises in the IFSC, Dublin City Centre. We have classes suitable to everyone from a complete beginner to an experienced grappler.

The school is run by Ryan Thompson, a 2nd degree black belt under Kurt Osiander, founder of Osiander BJJ in San Diego.

The core principle behind Celestial Jiu-Jitsu Academy can be expressed in one simple phrase: "Train hard and have fun!".

Here you can expect high-quality training, a friendly and welcoming atmosphere and mutual respect from everyone in the academy! Our doors are open to everyone - send us a message or pop over to the gym!
